Our Story

Prisha Industries —
Born in the Plains of Nepal

Prisha Industries was founded with a singular vision: to bring the rich, authentic flavours of Nepali spices to every household — from the Terai plains to mountain homes — at a price that respects the everyday consumer.

Based in Devdaha-1, Birta, Rupandehi, we are surrounded by the fertile agricultural heartland of Nepal. This gives us direct access to the finest raw spices grown under the shadow of the Himalayas.

Our brand Nepal Choice, under the Prisha umbrella, stands as a "Packet of Trust" — a promise that each sachet carries only pure, unadulterated spice, blended to perfection in our hygienic, modern facility.

We believe that great Nepali cooking deserves great Nepali spices. Our team of experienced blenders draws from generations of traditional recipes, adapting them for the modern kitchen without losing an ounce of authenticity.
